BSDLabs

FreeBSD Labs is a dedicated online space and initiative geared towards the realisation of community-driven THINK BIG and DIFFERENT ideas.

We want to brainstorm and prototype next-gen concepts that:

  • Can begin with a Prototype or MVP, then scale from there
  • Can be achieved with a small group (2-4) of diversely skilled people
  • Are not just related to “code” (src, ports, doc)
  • Are not just “feature requests”
  • Someone (YOU!) are willing to drive forward

Think “Skunk Works” for FreeBSD. Welcome to The Lab!
